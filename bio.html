I am a computational designer and VR/AR developer.

I am interested in the way the AEC industry can leverage new technology to create better designs. I have lived all over the world, and worked in several different architectural offices. I graduated with a B.Arch from Auburn University in 2016, with a year abroad in New Zealand. Recently I have participated in half a dozen Hackathons and won a awards at five of them.

I currently work primarily in JavaScript, C#, and Python. With JavaScript, I’ve designed and deployed numerous web applications and deployments leveraging THREE.js for front-end interfaces. With C#, I have developed a number of application add-ins, and other utilities with a specialization in AR/VR in Unity3d. With Python, I support firm-wide visual programming scripting and development workflows by offering training and support to the rest of the office.
